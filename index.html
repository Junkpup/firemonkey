Welcome stranger!
You have been tasked with putting out forest fires started by the evil Hooded Ones. Help the Fire Monkey save its home from the raging flames by collecting the buckets that are scattered across the perilous terrain. Click on the fire to put it out. Guide the Fire Monkey by using either or arrow keys. Stomp the evil Hooded Ones by jumping on them, and go forth to save the forest.

Controlled forest fires are considered beneficial for forest growth. However, uncontrolled, large-scale forest fires can put biodiversity at risk, cause air pollution and water pollution. A raging forest fire in Bandipur in the state of Karnataka, India wiped out 1000 hectares of forest in 2017.
